• The study components are entities needed when adding study data to the MPS-Db. They are the terms that are selected in dropdowns when setting up a study in the MPS-Db. All registered users can review study components here. Data providers (users who are assigned to, at least one, data provider group) can add new study components here, or by clicking on one of the provided throughout the study setup workflow.

    Component Description
    Assay Targets/Analytes Targets/analytes used to assess the response of a MPS model. Assay targets/analytes are associated to the method/kit used to measure response.
    Assay Method/Kits Assay method/kits used to measure assay targets/analytes.
    Measurement Types The type of measurement used to acquire an assay data point.
    Units of Measure Units available for quantifying values.
    Locations Locations within a device where treatments can be added and/or samples removed.
    Settings Settings used in describing the study design (e.g., flow rate, media used, etc.).
    Assay Suppliers The supplier of the kits and/or compounds used in performing the study.
    References Publications or other sources of information used in designing and/or performing a study.
    Component Description
    Devices Devices used in MPS models in the MPS-Db with the option to click on the device to view details of the device.
    MPS Models MPS Models in the MPS-Db with the option to click on the model and view details of the model's design.
    MPS Model Locations Locations within a device where treatments can be added and/or samples removed.
    Manufacturers Suppliers of MPS models and/or devices used to construct MPS Models.
    Component Description
    Compound Chemicals used in studies and/or features of the MPS-Db. Chemical properties are curated from reference sources.
    Component Description
    Cell Types The function of the cell.
    Cell Origins Indicator of hos the cells were isolated or acquired.
    Cell Samples Cell samples used in MPS models in the MPS-Db. The cell types and cell origins are included as part of the cell sample and must be added to the MPS-Db prior to adding the cell sample.
    Cell Biosensors Biosensors used in performing a study.
    Cell Suppliers The supplier of the cells used in performing the study.
